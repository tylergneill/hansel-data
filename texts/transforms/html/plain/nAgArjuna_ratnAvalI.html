§ 1 abhyudayanaiḥśreyasopāya

p.53, l.2

abhyudayanaiḥśreyasanirdeśo nāma prathamaḥ

p.54, l.1

oṃ namo ratnatrayāya ||

sarvadoṣavinirmuktaṃ guṇaiḥ sarvair alaṃkṛtam | praṇamya sarvajñam ahaṃ sarvasattvaikabāndhavam ||
dharmam ekāntakalyāṇaṃ rājan dharmodayāya te | vakṣyāmi dharmaḥ siddhiṃ hi yāti saddharmabhājane ||
prāg dharmābhyudayo yatra paścān naiḥśreyasodayaḥ | saṃprāpyābhyudayaṃ yasmād eti naiḥśreyasaṃ kramāt ||
sukham abhyudayas tatra mokṣo naiḥśreyaso mataḥ | asya sādhanasaṃkṣepaḥ śraddhāprajñe samāsataḥ ||
śrāddhatvād bhajate dharmaṃ prājñatvād vetti tattvataḥ | prajñā pradhānaṃ tv anayoḥ śraddhā pūrvaṅgamāsya tu ||
chandād dveṣād bhayān mohād yo dharmaṃ nātivartate | sa śrāddha iti vijñeyaḥ śreyaso bhājanaṃ param ||
kāyavāṅmānasaṃ karma sarvaṃ samyak parīkṣya yaḥ | parātmahitam ājñāya sadā kuryāt sa paṇḍitaḥ ||
ahiṃsā cauryaviratiḥ paradāravivarjanam | mithyāpaiśunyapāruṣyābaddhavādeṣu saṃyamaḥ ||
lobhavyāpādanāstikyadṛṣṭeś ca parivartanam | ete karmapathāḥ śuklā daśa kṛṣṇā viparyayāt ||
amadyapānaṃ svājīvo 'vihiṃsā dānam ādarāt | pūjyapūjā ca maitrī ca dharma eṣa samāsataḥ ||
śarīratāpanād dharmaḥ kevalān nāsti yat tataḥ | na paradrohaviratir na pareṣām anugrahaḥ ||
dānaśīlakṣamāspaṣṭaṃ yaḥ saddharmamahāpatham | anādṛtya vrajet kāyakleśagodaṇḍakotpathaiḥ ||
sa saṃsārāṭavīṃ ghorām anantajanapādapāṃ | kleśavyālāvalīḍhāṅgaḥ sudīrghaṃ pratipadyate ||
hiṃsayā jāyate 'lpāyur bahvābādho vihiṃsayā | cauryeṇa bhogavyasanī saśatruḥ pāradārikaḥ ||
abhyākhyānaṃ mṛṣāvādāt paiśunyān mitrabhedanam | apriyaśravaṇaṃ raukṣyād abaddhād durbhagaṃ vacaḥ ||
manorathān hanty abhidhyā vyāpādo bhayadaḥ smṛtaḥ | mithyādṛṣṭeḥ kudṛṣṭitvaṃ madyapānān matibhramaḥ ||
apradānena dāridryaṃ mithyājīvena vañcanā | stambhena duḥkulīnatvam alpaujaskatvam īrṣyayā ||
krodhād durvarṇatā maurkhyam apraśnena vipaścitām | phalam etan manuṣyatve sarvebhyaḥ prāk ca durgatiḥ ||
eṣāṃ akuśalākhyānāṃ vipāko yaḥ prakīrtitaḥ | kuśalānāñ ca sarveṣāṃ viparītaḥ phalodayaḥ ||
lobho dveṣaś ca mohaś ca tajjaṃ karmeti cāśubham | alobhādveṣāmohāś ca tajjaṃ karma ca tac chubham ||
aśubhāt sarvaduḥkhāni sarvā durgatayas tathā | śubhāt sugatayaḥ sarvāḥ sarvajanmasukhāni ca ||
nivṛttir aśubhāt kṛtsnāt pravṛttis tu śubhe sadā | kāyena manasā vācā dharmo 'yaṃ dvividhaḥ smṛtaḥ ||
narakapretatiryagbhyo dharmād asmād vimucyate | nṛṣu deveṣu cāpnoti sukhaśrīrājyavistarān ||
dhyānāpramāṇārūpyais tu brāhmādyaṃ sukham aśnute | ity abhyudayadharmo 'yaṃ phalaṃ cāsya samāsataḥ ||
naiḥśreyasaḥ punar dharmaḥ sūkṣmo gambhīradarśanaḥ | bālānām aśrutavatām uktaḥ trāsakaro jinaiḥ ||
nāsmy ahaṃ na bhaviṣyāmi na me 'sti na bhaviṣyati | iti bālasya saṃtrāsaḥ paṇḍitasya bhayakṣayaḥ ||
ahaṃkāraprasūteyaṃ mamakāropasaṃhitā | prajā prajāhitaikāntavādinābhihitākhilā ||
asty ahaṃ mama cāstīti mithyaitat paramārthataḥ | yathābhūtaparijñānān na bhavaty ubhayaṃ tataḥ ||
ahaṃkārodbhavāḥ skandhāḥ so 'haṃkāro 'nṛto 'rthataḥ | bījaṃ yasyānṛtaṃ tasya prarohaḥ satyataḥ kutaḥ ||
skandhān asatyān dṛṣṭvaivam ahaṃkāraḥ prahīyate | ahaṃkāraprahāṇāc ca na punaḥ skandhasaṃbhavaḥ ||
yathādarśam upādāya svamukhapratibimbakam | dṛśyate nāma tac caivam na kiṃcid api tattvataḥ ||
ahaṃkāras tathā skandhān upādāyopalabhyate | na ca kaścit sa tattvena svamukhapratibimbavat ||
yathādarśam anādāya svamukhapratibimbakam | na dṛśyate tathā skandhān anādāyāham ity api ||
evaṃvidhārthaśravaṇād dharmacakṣur avāptavān | āryānandaḥ svayaṃ caiva bhikṣubhyo 'bhīkṣṇam uktavān ||
skandhagrāho yāvad asmāt tāvad evāham ity api | ahaṃkāre sati punaḥ karma janma tataḥ punaḥ ||
trivartmaitad anādyantamadhyaṃ saṃsāramaṇḍalam | ālātamaṇḍalaprakhyaṃ bhramaty anyonyahetukam ||
svaparobhayatas tasya traikālye cāpy alabdhitaḥ | ahaṃkāraḥ kṣayaṃ yāti tataḥ karma ca janma ca ||
evaṃ hetuphalotpādaṃ paśyaṃs tatkṣayam eva ca | nāstitām astitāṃ caiva naiti lokasya tattvataḥ ||
sarvaduḥkhakṣayaṃ dharmaṃ śrutvaivam aparīkṣakaḥ | saṃtrasyaty aparijñānād abhayasthānakātaraḥ ||
na bhaviṣyati nirvāṇe sarvam etan na te bhayam | ucyamāna ihābhāvas tasya te kiṃ bhayaṃkaraḥ ||
mokṣe nātmā na ca skandhā mokṣaś ced īdṛśaḥ priyaḥ | ātmaskandhāpanayanaṃ kim ihaiva na te priyam ||
na cābhāvo 'pi nirvāṇaṃ kuta evāsya bhāvatā | bhāvābhāvaparāmarśakṣayo nirvāṇam ucyate ||
samāsān nāstitādṛṣṭiḥ phalaṃ nāstīti karmaṇām | apuṇyāpāyikī caiṣā mithyādṛṣṭir iti smṛtā ||
samāsād astitādṛṣṭiḥ phalaṃ cāstīti karmaṇām | puṇyā sugatiniṣyandā samyagdṛṣṭir iti smṛtā ||
jñānān nāstyastitāśānteḥ pāpapuṇyavyatikramaḥ | durgateḥ sugateś cāsmāt sa mokṣaḥ sadbhir ucyate ||
sahetum udayaṃ paśyan nāstitām ativartate | astitām api nopaiti nirodhaṃ saha hetunā ||
prāgjātaḥ sahajātaś ca hetur āhetuko 'rthataḥ | prajñapter apratītatvād utpatteś caiva tattvataḥ ||
asmin satīdaṃ bhavati dīrghe hrasvaṃ yathā sati | asyotpādād udetīdaṃ dīpotpādād yathā prabhā ||
hrasve 'sati punar dīrghaṃ na bhavaty asvabhāvataḥ | pradīpasyāpy anutpādāt prabhāyā apy asaṃbhavaḥ ||
evaṃ hetuphalotpādaṃ dṛṣṭvā nopaiti nāstitām | abhyupetyāsya lokasya yāthābhūtyaṃ prapañcajam ||
nirodhañ cāprapañcotthaṃ yāthābhūtyād upāgatam | nopayāty astitāṃ tasmān mucyate 'dvayaniśritaḥ ||
dūrād ālokitaṃ rūpam āsannairdṛśyate sphuṭam | marīcir yadi vāri syād āsannaiḥ kiṃ na dṛśyate ||
dūrībhūtair yathābhūto loko 'yaṃ dṛśyate tathā | na dṛśyate tadāsannair animitto marīcivat ||
marīcis toyasadṛśī yathā nāmbho na cārthataḥ | skandhās tathātmasadṛśā nātmāno nāpi te 'rthataḥ ||
marīciṃ toyam ity etad iti matvā gato 'trasan | yadi nāstīti tat toyaṃ gṛhṇīyān mūḍha eva saḥ ||
marīcipratimaṃ lokam evam astīti gṛhṇataḥ | nāstīti cāpi moho 'yaṃ sati mohe na mucyate ||
nāstiko durgatiṃ yāti sugatiṃ yāti cāstikaḥ | yathābhūtaparijñānān mokṣam advayaniśritaḥ ||
anicchan nāstitāstitve yathābhūtaparijñayā | nāstitāṃ labhate mohāt kasmān na labhate 'stitām ||
syād astidūṣaṇād asya nāstitākṣipyate 'rthataḥ | nāstitādūṣanād evaṃ kasmān nākṣipyate 'stitā ||
na pratijñā na caritaṃ na cittaṃ bodhiniśrayāt | nāstikatve 'rthato yeṣāṃ kathaṃ te nāstikāḥ smṛṭāḥ ||
sasāṃkhyaulūkyanirgranthapudgalaskandhavādinam | pṛccha lokaṃ yadi vadaty astināstivyatikramaṃ ||
dharmayautakam ity asmān nāstyastitvavyatikramam | viddhi gambhīram ity uktaṃ buddhānāṃ śāsanāmṛtam ||
vibhavaṃ naiti nāyāti na tiṣṭhaty api ca kṣaṇam | traikālyavyativṛttātmā loka evaṃ kuto 'rthataḥ ||
dvayor apy āgatigatī yat sthitiś ca na tattvataḥ | lokanirvāṇayos tasmād viśeṣaḥ ka ivārthataḥ ||
sthiter abhāvād udayo nirodhaś ca na tattvataḥ | uditaś ca sthitaś ceti niruddhaś ca kuto 'rthataḥ ||
katham aksaṇiko bhāvaḥ pariṇāmaḥ sadā yadi | nāsti cet pariṇāmaḥ syād anyathātvaṃ kuto 'rthataḥ ||
ekadeśe kṣayād vā syāt kṣaṇikaṃ sarvaśo 'pi vā | vaiṣamyānupalabdheś ca dvidhāpy etad ayuktimat ||
kṣaṇike sarvathābhāvāt kutaḥ kācit purāṇatā | sthairyād akṣaṇike cāpi kutaḥ kācit purāṇatā ||
yathānto 'sti kṣaṇasyaivam ādimadhyaṃ ca kalpyatām | tryātmakatvāt kṣaṇasyaivaṃ na lokasya kṣaṇaṃ sthitiḥ ||
ādimadhyāvasānāni cintyāni kṣaṇavat punaḥ | ādimadhyāvasānatvaṃ na svataḥ parato 'pi vā ||
naiko 'nekapradeśatvān nāpradeśaś ca kaścana | vinaikam api nāneko nāstitvam api cāstitām ||
vināśāt pratipakṣād vā syād astitvasya nāstitā | vināśaḥ pratipakṣo vā kathaṃ syād astyasaṃbhavāt ||
nirvṛtes tena lokasya nopaity ūnatvam arthataḥ | antavān iti lokaś ca pṛṣṭas tūṣṇīṃ jino 'bhavat ||
sarvajña iti sarvajño budhais tenaiva gamyate | yenaitad dharmagāmbhīryaṃ novācābhājane jane ||
iti naiḥśreyaso dharmo gambhīro niṣparigrahaḥ | anālaya iti proktaḥ saṃbuddhais tattvadarśibhiḥ ||
asmād anālayād dharmād ālayābhiratā janāḥ | astināstyavyatikrāntā bhītā naśyanty amedhasaḥ ||
te naṣṭā nāśayanty anyān abhayasthānabhīravaḥ | tathā kuru yathā rājaṃs tair naṣṭair nopanāśyase ||
tasya te 'vipraṇāśāya rājaṃl lokottaraṃ nayaṃ | yathābhūtāgamavaśād vakṣyāmy advayaniśritam ||
pāpapuṇyakriyātītam gambhīraṃ vivṛtārthavat | anyatīrthair anālīḍhaṃ svaiś cāniśrayabhīrubhiḥ ||
na bhūmiḥ puruśo nāpo na tejo nānilo na kham | na vijñānaṃ na sarvāṇi tebhyo 'nyaḥ ko 'sty ataḥ pumān ||
ṣaḍdhātuḥ puruṣo yadvat saṃghātatvān na tattvataḥ | dhātur apy evam ekaikaḥ saṃghātatvān na tattvataḥ ||
nātmā skandhā na te tasmin na sa teṣu na tair vinā | nāpy agnīndhanavan miśraḥ skandhair astīty ataḥ katham ||
bhūr na bhūtatrayaṃ nāsyāṃ neyaṃ tatra na tair vinā | ekaikam evam ity asmād bhūtāny apy ātmavan mṛṣā ||
pṛthivyagnyambuvāyūnām ekaikaṃ na svabhāvataḥ | ekaikaṃ yad vinā nāsti tribhir ekena ca trayam ||
ekaikaṃ yad vinā nāsti tribhir ekena ca trayaṃ | pratisvam asatām eṣāṃ saṃghāto jāyate kathaṃ ||
atha pratisvaṃ vidyante kiṃ nāsty agnir vinendhanam | calapratighasaṃghātais toyavāyubhuvas tathā ||
atha agnir apratisvo 'yaṃ pratisvaṃ tat kathaṃ trayam | pratītyotpādavaidharmyas trayāṇāṃ tu na yujyate ||
pratisvaṃ vidyamānānāṃ kiṃ sattaiṣāṃ parasparāt | pratisvam asatāṃ caiṣāṃ kathaṃ sattā parasparāt ||
yatraikaṃ tatra śiṣṭāni pratisvaṃ santi naiva cet | naikasthatvam amiśrāṇāṃ miśrāṇāṃ na pratisvatā ||
bhūtānām apratisvānāṃ pratisvaṃ lakṣaṇaṃ kutaḥ | nāneko 'py apratisvānāṃ saṃvṛtyoktaṃ tu lakṣaṇam ||
varṇagandharasasparśā ye teṣām apy ayaṃ nayaḥ | cakṣurvijñānarūpāṇām avidyākarmajanmanām ||
kartṛkarmakriyāsaṃkhyāyogahetuphalādhvanām | hrasvadīrghādisaṃjñānāṃ saṃjñāsaṃjñitayos tathā ||
pṛthivītoyavāyvagnisthūlahrasvāṇudīrghatā | śubhādyaṃ caiva vijñāne muninoktaṃ nirudhyate ||
anidarśanaṃ ca vijñānam anantaṃ sarvataḥ prabhuḥ | atrāpaḥ pṛthivī caiva tejo vāyur na gāhate ||
atra hrasvaṃ ca dīrghaṃ cāṇusthūlaṃ śubhāśubham | atra nāma ca rūpaṃ ca niḥśeṣam avarudhyate ||
etat sarvaṃ yad ajñānād vijñāne pūrvam utthitam | vijñāna eva tad jñānād evaṃ paścān nirudhyate ||
vijñānāgner jagaddharmāḥ sarva evendhanaṃ matāḥ | tān yathāvat pravicayajvālair dagdhvopaśāmyate ||
ajñānakalpitaṃ pūrvaṃ paścāt tattvārthaniścaye | yadā na labhate bhāvam evābhāvaṃ tadā kutaḥ ||
rūpasyābhāvamātratvād ākāśaṃ nāmamātrakam | bhūtair vinā kuto rūpaṃ nāmamātratvam apy asat ||
saṃskāravedanāsaṃjñāvijñānāny api bhūtavat | cintyāny ātmavad ity asmād dhātuṣaṭkam anātmakam ||

§ 2 miśraka

p.96, l.2

miśrako nāma dvitīyaḥ

kadalī pāṭitā yadvan niḥśeṣāvayavaiḥ saha | na kiṃcit puruṣas tadvat pāṭitaḥ saha dhātubhiḥ ||
sarvadharmā anātmāna ity asmād bhāṣitaṃ jinaiḥ | dhātuṣaṭkaṃ ca taiḥ sarvaṃ nirṇītaṃ tac ca nārthataḥ ||
naivam ātmā na cānātmā yāthābhūtena labhyate | ātmānātmakṛte dṛṣṭī vavārāsmān mahāmuniḥ ||
dṛṣṭaśrutādyaṃ muninā na satyaṃ na mṛṣoditam | pakṣād dhi pratipakṣaḥ syād ubhayaṃ tac ca nārthataḥ ||
iti satyānṛtātīto loko 'yaṃ paramārthataḥ | asmād eva ca tattvena nopaity asti ca nāsti ca ||
yac caivaṃ sarvathā neti sarvajñas tat kathaṃ vadet | sāntam ity athavānantaṃ dvayaṃ vādvayam eva vā ||
asaṃkhyeyā gatā buddhās tathaiṣyanty atha sāṃpratāḥ | koṭyagraśaś ca sattvāntas tebhyas traikālyajo mataḥ ||
vṛddhihetur na lokasya kṣayas traikālyasambhavaḥ | sarvajñena kathaṃ tasya pūrvānto 'vyākṛtaḥ kṛtaḥ ||
etat tad dharmagāmbhīryaṃ yat tad guhyaṃ pṛthagjane | māyopamatvaṃ lokasya buddhānāṃ śāsanāmṛtam ||
māyāgajasya dṛśyeta yathā janmānta eva ca | na ca kaścit sa tattvena janmāntaś caiva vidyate ||
māyopamasya lokasya tathā janmānta eva ca | dṛśyate paramārthena na ca janmānta eva ca ||
yathā māyāgajo naiti kutaścid yāti na kvacit | cittamohanamātratvād bhāvatvena na tiṣṭhati ||
tathā māyopamo loko naiti yāti na kutracit | cittamohanamātratvād bhāvatvena na tiṣṭhati ||
traikālyavyativṛttātmā loka evaṃ nu ko 'rthataḥ | yo 'sti nāsty athavāpi syād anyatra vyavahārataḥ ||
catuṣprakāram ity asmāt sānto 'nanto dvayo 'dvayaḥ | buddhena hetor nānyasmād ayam avyākṛtaḥ kṛtaḥ ||
śarīrāśucitā tāvat sthūlā pratyakṣagocarā | satataṃ dṛśyamānāpi yadā citte na tiṣṭhati ||
tadātisūkṣmo gambhīraḥ saddharmo 'yaṃ anālayaḥ | apratyakṣaḥ kathaṃ citte sukhenāvatariṣyati ||
sambudhyāsmān nivṛtto 'bhūd dharmaṃ deśayituṃ muniḥ | durjñānam atigāmbhīryāt jñātvā dharmam imaṃ janaiḥ ||
vināśayati durjñāto dharmo 'yam avipaścitam | nāstitādṛṣṭisamale yasmād asmin nimajjati ||
aparo 'py asya durjñānān mūrkhaḥ paṇḍitamānikaḥ | pratikṣepavinaṣṭātmā yāty avīcim adhomukhaḥ ||
durbhuktena yathānnena vināśam adhigacchati | subhuktenāyur ārogyaṃ balaṃ saukhyāni cāśnute ||
durjñātena tathānena vināśam adhigacchati | samyagjñātena tu sukhaṃ bodhiṃ cāpnoty anuttarām ||
tasmād atra pratikṣepaṃ dṛṣṭiṃ tyaktvā ca nāstikīm | samyagjñānaparaṃ yatnaṃ kuru sarvārthasiddhaye ||
dharmasyāsyāparijñānād ahaṃkāro 'nuvartate | tataḥ śubhāśubhaṃ karma tato janma śubhāśubham ||
tasmād yāvad avijñāto dharmo 'haṃkāraśātanaḥ | dānaśīlakṣamādharme tāvad ādaravān bhava ||
dharmapūrvāṇi kāryāṇi dharmamadhyāni pārthiva | sādhayan dharmaniṣṭhāni neha nāmutra sīdati ||
dharmāt kīrtiḥ sukhaṃ caiva neha bhīr na mumūrṣataḥ | paraloke sukhaṃ sphītaṃ tasmād dharmaṃ sadā bhaja ||
dharma eva parā nītir dharmāl loko 'bhirajyate | rañjitena hi lokena neha nāmutra vañcyate ||
adharmeṇa tu yā nītis tayā loko 'parajyate | lokāparañjanāc caiva neha nāmutra nandati ||
parātisaṃdhānaparā kaṣṭā durgatipaddhatiḥ | anarthavidyā duṣprajñair arthavidyā kathaṃ kṛtā ||
parātisaṃdhānaparo nītimān katham arthataḥ | yena janmasahasrāṇi bahūny ātmaiva vañcyate ||
ripor apriyam anvicchan doṣāṃs tyaktvā guṇān śraya | svahitāvāptir evaṃ te ripoś cāpy apriyaṃ bhavet ||
dānena priyavadyena hitenaikārthacaryayā | ebhir ācara lokasya dharmasyaiva ca saṃgraham ||
viśvāsaṃ janayaty ekaṃ satyaṃ rājñāṃ yathā dṛḍham | tathaivānṛtam apy eṣām aviśvāsakaraṃ param ||
na visaṃvādavat satyaṃ na bhāvodgatam arthataḥ | paraikāntahitaṃ satyam ahitatvān mṛṣetarat ||
doṣān pracchādayaty ekas tyāgo rājñāṃ yathojjvalaḥ | tathā kārpaṇyam apy eṣāṃ guṇasarvasvaghātakam ||
upaśāntasya gāmbhīryaṃ gāmbhīryād gauravaṃ param | gauravād dīptir ājñā ca tasmād upaśamaṃ bhaja ||
ahāryabuddhiḥ prājñatvād aparapratyayaḥ sthiraḥ | nātisaṃdhīyate rājā tasmāt prajñāparo bhava ||
satyatyāgaśamaprajñācaturbhadro narādhipaḥ | dharmaś caturbhadra iva stūyate devamānuṣaiḥ ||
nigṛhyavādibhiḥ śuddhaiḥ prajñākāruṇyanirmalaiḥ | sahāsīnasya satataṃ prajñā dharmaś ca vardhate ||

mātāpitṛbharo jantuḥ kulajyeṣṭhāpacāyakaḥ | satsaṃbhogī kṣamī tyāgī ślakṣṇāpiśunasatyavāk ||
vratād ā maraṇād asmād devendratvaṃ samāpnuyāt | karoty adyāpi cendratvaṃ tasmād dharmāsanaṃ bhaja ||
traikālyadattād ekāhe sthālapākaśatatrayāt | puṇyaṃ mauhūrtikasyaiti na maitrasya kalām api ||
priyo devamanuṣyāṇāṃ bhavaty etaiś ca rakṣyate | saumanasyasukhī prāyo 'viṣaśastrena bādhyate ||
arthān ayatnāl labhate brahmaloke ca jāyate | maitrīdharmān imān aṣṭau bhuṅkte yadi na mucyate ||
cittāny utpādya sattvānāṃ bodhau kṛtvā sthirāṇi ca | bodhicittam avāpnoti sthiraṃ śailendrarājavat ||
akṣaṇān śraddhayā naiti śīlād yāti śubhāṃ gatiṃ | labhate śūnyatābhyāsāt kuśaleṣv apramattatām ||
aśaṭhaḥ smṛtim āpnoti matim āpnoti cintakaḥ | gauravād arthagatimān prājñaḥ syād dharmadhāraṇāt ||
dharmaśravaṇadānānāṃ kṛtvā nāvaraṇakriyām | labhate kṣipram eveṣṭāṃ buddhaiḥ samavadhānatām ||

upānadyānadānena durbalodvahanena ca | vahanād gurumānyānāṃ viduṣām ṛddhir āpyate ||

§ 4 rājavṛttopadeśa

p.183, l.2

rājavṛttopadeśo nāma caturthaḥ |

adharmam anyāyyam api prāyo rājānujīvibhiḥ | ācaraṃ stūyate yasmāt kṛcchrād vetti kṣamākṣamam ||
anyo 'pi tāvad yaḥ kaścid durvacaḥ kṣemam apriyam | kim u rājā mahābhaumas tvaṃ mayā bhikṣuṇā satā ||
tvatkṛtād eva tu snehāj jagatām anukampayā | aham eko vadāmi tvāṃ pathyam apy apriyaṃ bhṛśam ||
satyaṃ ślakṣṇārthavat pathyaṃ śiṣyaḥ kāle 'nukampayā | vācya ity āha bhagavāṃs tad evam abhidhīyase ||
akrodhe satyavākye ca śrāvyamāṇo 'pi tu sthitaḥ | sevyaṃ saṃparigṛhṇīyāt sattoyaṃ snāpyamānavat ||
tasya me vadato vākyaṃ tvam ihāmutra ca kṣamam | jñātvā kuru hitāyedam ātmano jagato 'pi ca ||
yācakebhyaḥ purā dānāt prāpyārthāṃś cen na dāsyasi | akṛtajñatvalobhābhyāṃ nārthān punar avāpsyasi ||
iha pathyadanaṃ loke na vahaty abhṛto bhṛtaḥ | yācakas tv abhṛto 'mutra hīnaḥ śataguṇodvahaḥ ||
udāracittaḥ satataṃ bhavodārakriyārataḥ | udārakarmaṇaḥ sarvam udāraṃ jāyate phalam ||
manorathair api klībair anālīḍhaṃ narādhipaiḥ | kuru dharmāspadaṃ śrīmat khyātaṃ ratnatrayāspadam ||
sāmantarājaromāñcakaraṃ dharmāspadaṃ na yat | mṛtasyāpy apraśasyatvād rājaṃs tad akṛtaṃ varam ||
atyaudāryād udārāṇāṃ vismayotsāhavardhanam | utsāhaghnaṃ ca mandānāṃ sarvasvenāpi kāraya ||
utsṛjyāmutra gantavyaṃ sarvasvam avaśena te | dharme niyuktaṃ yāty eva purastāt sarvam eva te ||
sarvasvaṃ pūrvanṛpater nṛpasya vaśam āgatam | kiṃ pūrvakasya dharmāya sukhāya yaśase 'pi vā ||
bhuktād arthād iha sukhaṃ dattāt pāratrikaṃ sukham | abhuktādattanaṣṭāt tu duḥkham eva kutaḥ sukham ||
vinaśyan sacivair dātum asvātantryān na śakyasi | āyaticchedaniḥsnehair navarājapriyaiṣibhiḥ ||
sarvasvenāpy ataḥ svasthaḥ śīghraṃ dharmāspadaṃ kuru | mṛtyupratyayamadhyasthaḥ pravātasthapradīpavat ||
dharmādhikārā ye cānye pūrvarājapravartitāḥ | devadroṇyādayas te 'pi pravartyantāṃ yathā sthitāḥ ||
ahiṃsakaiḥ śubhācārair vratasthair atithipriyaiḥ | sarvakṣamair akalahair bhajyeraṃs taiḥ sadodyataiḥ ||
andhavyādhitahīnāṅgadīnānāthavanīpakāḥ | te 'py annapānaṃ sāmyena labherann avighaṭṭitāḥ ||
anarthinām api satāṃ dhārmikāṇām anugrahān | apy anyarājyasaṃsthānām anurūpān pravartaya ||
sarvadharmādhikāreṣu dharmādhikṛtam utthitam | alubdhaṃ paṇḍitaṃ dharmyaṃ kuru teṣām abādhakam ||
nītijñān dhārmikān snigdhān śucīn bhaktān akātarān | kulinān śīlasampannān kṛtajñān sacivān kuru ||
akṣudrāṃs tyāginaḥ śūrān snigdhān sambhoginaḥ sthirān | kuru nityāpramattāṃs ca dhārmikān daṇḍanāyakān ||
dharmaśīlān śucīn dakṣān kāryajñān śāstrakovidān | kṛtavṛttīn samān snigdhān vṛddhān adhikṛtān kuru ||
pratimāsaṃ ca tebhyas tvaṃ sarvam āyavyayaṃ śṛṇu | śrutvā dharmādhikārādyaṃ kāryaṃ sarvaṃ svayaṃ vada ||
dharmārthaṃ yadi te rājyaṃ na kīrtyarthaṃ na kāmataḥ | tataḥ saphalam atyartham anarthārtham ato 'nyathā ||
parasparāmiṣībhūte loke 'smin prāyaśo nṛpa | yathā rājyaṃ ca dharmaś ca bhavet tava tathā śṛṇu ||
jñānavṛddhāḥ kule jātā nyāyajñāḥ pāpabhīravaḥ | sametā bahavo nityaṃ santu te kāryadarśinaḥ ||
daṇḍabandhaprahārādīn kuryus te nyāyato 'pi cet | kāruṇyārdraḥ sadā bhūtvā tvam anugrahavān bhava ||
hitāyaiva tvayā cittam unnāmyaṃ sarvadehinām | kāruṇyāt satataṃ rājaṃs tīvrapāpakṛtām api ||
tīvrapāpeṣu hiṃsreṣu kṛpā kāryā viśeṣataḥ | tatreva hi kṛpāpātraṃ hatātmāno mahātmanām ||
pratyahaṃ pañcarātraṃ vā baddhān kṣīṇān vimocaya | śeṣān api yathāyogaṃ mā kāṃścit naiva mocaya ||
yeṣv amokṣaṇacittaṃ te jāyate teṣv asaṃvaraḥ | tasmād asaṃvarāt pāpam ajasram upacīyate ||
yāvac ca na vimucyeraṃs tāvat syuḥ sukhabandhanāḥ | nāpitasnānapānānnabhaiṣajyavasanānvitāḥ ||
apātreṣv iva putreṣu pātrīkaraṇakāṅkṣayā | kāruṇyāc chāsanaṃ kāryaṃ na dveṣān nārthalipsayā ||
vimṛśya samyag vijñāya praduṣṭān ghātakān api | ahatvāpīḍayitvā ca kuru nirviṣayān narān ||
svatantraḥ paśya sarvaṃ ca viṣayaṃ cāracakṣuṣā | nityāpramattaḥ smṛtimān kuru kāryaṃ ca dhārmikam ||
pradānamānasatkārair guṇasthān satataṃ bhaja | udārair anurūpais tu śeṣān api yathāvidhi ||
saṃmānasphītakusumaḥ saṃpradānamahāphalaḥ | rājavṛkṣaḥ kṣamācchāyaḥ sevyate bhṛtyapakṣibhiḥ ||
tyāgaśīlamayo rājā tejasvī bhavati priyaḥ | śarkarāmodako yadvad elāmaricakarkaśaḥ ||
mātsyanyāyaś ca te naivaṃ nyāyād rājyaṃ bhaviṣyati | na cānyāyo na vādharmo dharmaś caivaṃ bhaviṣyati ||
paralokāt tvayā rājyaṃ nānītaṃ nāpi neṣyasi | dharmāt prāptam ato 'syārthe nādharmaṃ kartum arhasi ||
rājyena bhāṇḍamūlyena duḥkhabhāṇḍaparaṃparām | rājan yathā nārjayasi prayatnaḥ kriyatāṃ tathā ||
rājyena bhāṇḍamūlyena rājyabhāṇḍaparaṃparām | rājan yathā nirviśasi prayatnaḥ kriyatāṃ tathā ||
caturdvīpām api prāpya pṛthivīṃ cakravartinaḥ | śārīraṃ mānasaṃ caiva sukhadvayam idaṃ matam ||
duḥkhapratikriyāmātraṃ śārīraṃ vedanāsukhaṃ | saṃjñāmayaṃ mānasaṃ tu kevalaṃ kalpanākṛtam ||
duḥkhapratikriyāmātraṃ kalpanāmātram eva ca | lokasya sukhasarvasvaṃ vyartham etad ato 'rthataḥ ||
dvīpadeśapurāvāsapradeśāsanavāsasām | śayyānnapānahastyaśvastrīṇāṃ caikaikabhogyatā ||
yadā ca yatra cittaṃ syāt tadā tena sukhaṃ kila | śeṣāṇām amanaskārāt teṣāṃ vyarthatvam arthataḥ ||
viṣayān pañcabhiḥ pañca cakṣurādibhir indriyaiḥ | na kalpayati yad gṛhṇan nāsmāt teṣu tadā sukham ||
jānīte viṣayaṃ yaṃ yaṃ yena yenendriyeṇa ca | tadā na śeṣaiḥ śeṣāṇi vyarthāny eva yatas tadā ||
indriyair upalabdhasya viṣayasyākṛtiṃ manaḥ | upalabhya vyatītasya kalpayan manyate sukham ||
ekam arthaṃ vijānāti yady apy ekam ihendriyam | tad apy arthaṃ vinā vyarthaṃ vyartho 'rtho 'pi ca tad vinā ||
pratītya mātāpitarau yathoktaḥ putrasaṃbhavaḥ | cakṣūrūpe pratītyaivam ukto vijñānasaṃbhavaḥ ||
atītānāgatā vyarthā viṣayāḥ sārdham indriyaiḥ | taddvayānatiriktatvād vyarthā ye 'pi ca sāmpratāḥ ||
alātacakraṃ gṛhṇāti yathā cakṣur viparyayāt | tathendriyāṇi gṛhṇanti viṣayān sāṃpratān iva ||
indriyāṇīndriyārthāś ca pañcabhūtamayā matāḥ | pratisvaṃ bhūtavaiyarthyād eṣāṃ vyarthatvam arthataḥ ||
nirindhano 'gnir bhūtānāṃ vinirbhāge prasajyate | saṃparke lakṣaṇābhāvaḥ śeṣeṣv apy eṣa nirṇayaḥ ||
evaṃ dvidhāpi bhūtānāṃ vyarthatvāt saṅgatir vṛthā | vyarthatvāt saṅgateś caiva rūpaṃ vyartham ato 'rthataḥ ||
vijñānavedanāsaṃjñāsaṃskārāṇāṃ ca sarvaśaḥ | pratyekam ātmavaiyarthyād vaiyarthyaṃ paramārthataḥ ||
sukhābhimāno duḥkhasya pratīkāre yathārthataḥ | tathā duḥkhābhimāno 'pi sukhasya pratighātajaḥ ||
sukhe saṃyogatṛṣṇaivaṃ naiḥsvābhāvyāt prahīyate | duḥkhe viyogatṛṣṇā ca paśyatāṃ muktir ity ataḥ ||
kaḥ paśyatīti cec cittaṃ vyavahāreṇa kathyate | na hi caittaṃ vinā cittaṃ vyarthatvān na saheṣyate ||
vyartham evaṃ jagan matvā yāthābhūtā nirāspadā | nirvāti nirupādāno nirupādānavahnivat ||
bodhisattvo 'pi dṛṣṭvaivaṃ saṃbodhau niyato mataḥ | kevalaṃ tv asya kāruṇyād ā bodher bhavasaṃtatiḥ ||
bodhisattvasya saṃbhāro mahāyāne tathāgataiḥ | nirdiṣṭaḥ sa tu saṃmūḍhaiḥ pradviṣṭaiś caiva nindyate ||
guṇadoṣānabhijño vā doṣasaṃjñī guṇeṣu vā | athavāpi guṇadveṣī mahāyānasya nindakaḥ ||
paropaghātino doṣān parānugrāhiṇo guṇān | jñātvocyate guṇadveṣī mahāyānasya nindakaḥ ||
yat svārthanirapekṣatvāt parārthaikarasapriyam | guṇākaraṃ mahāyānaṃ tad dveṣī tena dahyate ||
śrāddho 'pi durgṛhītena dviṣyāt kruddho 'thavetaraḥ | śrāddho 'pi dagdha ity uktaḥ kā cintā dveṣabandhure ||
viṣeṇāpi viṣaṃ hanyād yathaivoktaṃ cikitsakaiḥ | duḥkhenāpy ahitaṃ hanyād ity ukte kiṃ virudhyate ||
manaḥpūrvaṃgamā dharmā manaḥśreṣṭhā iti śruteḥ | hitaṃ hitamanāḥ kurvan duḥkhenāpy ahitaṃ katham ||
duḥkham apy āyatīpathyaṃ kāryaṃ kim u sukhaṃ hitam | ātmanaś ca pareṣāṃ ca dharma eṣa sanātanaḥ ||
mātrāsukhaparityāgāt paścāc ced vipulaṃ sukham | tyajen mātrāsukhaṃ dhīraḥ saṃpaśyanvipulaṃ sukham ||
na mṛṣyate ca yady etat kaṭubhaiṣajyadāyinaḥ | tataś cikitsakādyāś ca hatā naivaṃ ca yujyate ||
apathyam api yad dṛṣṭaṃ tat pathyaṃ paṇḍitaiḥ kvacit | utsargaś cāpavādaś ca sarvaśāstreṣu śasyate ||
karuṇāpūrvakāḥ sarve niṣyandā jñānanirmalāḥ | uktā yatra mahāyāne kas tan nindet sacetanaḥ ||
atyaudāryātigāmbhīryād viṣaṇṇair akṛtātmabhiḥ | nindyate 'dya mahāyānaṃ mohāt svaparavairibhiḥ ||
dānaśīlakṣamāvīryadhyānaprajñākṛpātmakam | mahāyānam atas tasmin kasmād durbhāṣitaṃ vacaḥ ||
parārtho dānaśīlābhyāṃ kṣāntyā vīryeṇa cātmanaḥ | dhyānaṃ prajñā ca mokṣāya mahāyānārthasaṃgrahaḥ ||
parātmahitamokṣārthāḥ saṃkṣepād buddhaśāsanam | te ṣaṭpāramitāgarbhās tasmād bauddham idaṃ vacaḥ ||
puṇyajñānamayo yatra buddhair bodhimahāpathaḥ | deśitas tan mahāyānam ajñānāndhe na mṛṣyate ||
kham ivācintyapuṇyatvād ukto 'cintyaguṇo jinaḥ | mahāyāne yato buddhamāhātmyaṃ kṣamyatām idam ||
āryaśāradvatasyāpi śīlamātre 'py agocaraḥ | yasmāt tad buddhamāhātmyam acintyaṃ kiṃ na mṛṣyate ||
anutpādo mahāyāne pareṣāṃ śūnyatā kṣayaḥ | kṣayānutpādayoś caikyam arthataḥ kṣamyatāṃ yataḥ ||
śūnyatābuddhamāhātmyam evaṃ yuktyānupaśyatām | mahāyānetaroktāni na sameyuḥ kathaṃ satām ||
tathāgatābhisaṃdhyoktāny asukhaṃ jñātum ity ataḥ | ekayānatriyānoktād ātmā rakṣya upekṣayā ||
upekṣayā hi nāpuṇyam dveṣāt pāpaṃ kutaḥ śubham | mahāyāne yato dveṣo nātmakāmaiḥ kṛto 'rhati ||
na bodhisattvapraṇidhir na caryāpariṇāmanā | uktāḥ śrāvakayāne 'smād bodhisattvas tataḥ kutaḥ ||
adhiṣṭhānāni noktāni bodhisattvasya bodhaye | buddhair anyat pramāṇaṃ ca ko 'sminn arthe jinādhikaḥ ||
adhiṣṭhānāryasatyārthabodhipakṣopasaṃhitāt | mārgāc chrāvakasāmānyād bauddhaṃ kenādhikaṃ phalam ||
bodhicaryāpratiṣṭhārthaṃ na sūtre bhāṣitaṃ vacaḥ | bhāṣitaṃ ca mahāyāne grāhyam asmād vicakṣaṇaiḥ ||
yathaiva vaiyākaraṇo mātṛkām api pāṭhayet | buddho 'vadat tathā dharmaṃ vineyānāṃ yathākṣamam ||
keṣāṃcid avadad dharmaṃ pāpebhyo vinivṛttaye | keṣāṃcit puṇyasiddhyarthaṃ keṣāṃcid dvayaniśritam ||
dvayāniśritam ekeṣāṃ gambhīraṃ bhīrubhīṣaṇam | śūnyatākaruṇāgarbham ekeṣāṃ bodhisādhanam ||
iti sadbhir mahāyāne kartavyaḥ pratighakṣayaḥ | prasādaś cādhikaḥ kāryaḥ samyaksaṃbodhisiddhaye ||
mahāyānaprasādena taduktācaraṇena ca | prāpyate 'nuttarā bodhiḥ sarvasaukhyāni cāntarā ||
dānaśīlakṣamāsatyaṃ gṛhasthasya viśeṣataḥ | dharma uktaḥ kṛpāgarbhaḥ sa sātmīkriyatāṃ dṛḍham ||
atha lokasya vaidharmyād rājyaṃ dharmeṇa duṣkaram | tato dharmayaśo 'rthaṃ te pravrajyādhigamaḥ kṣamaḥ ||

§ 5 pañcama = *bodhisattvācāra

p.229, l.2

pañcamaḥ paricchedaḥ

tataḥ pravrajitenādau kāryaḥ śikṣādaraḥ paraḥ | pratimokṣe savinaye bāhuśrutye 'rthanirṇaye ||
tato doṣāḥ prahātavyāḥ kṣudravastukasaṃjñitāḥ | yatnena saptapañcāśat kīrtyamānān nibodha tān ||

māyeti vañcanā śāṭhyaṃ cittasaṃtānajihmatā | īrṣyā paraguṇais tāpo mātsaryaṃ tyāgabhīrutā ||
ahrīkatānapatrāpye svapareṣām alajjane | asaṃnatikṛtaḥ stambhaḥ saṃrambhaḥ kopavibhramaḥ ||
mado darpaḥ pramādas tu kuśaleṣv aprayogitā | mānaḥ punaḥ saptavidhas taṃ vakṣyāmi prabhedataḥ ||
tatrābhimanyamānasya hīnād dhīnaṃ samāt samaṃ | hīnād vādhikam ātmānaṃ samam vā māna ucyate ||
yo 'dhamas tulyam ātmānaṃ viśiṣṭād abhimanyate | so 'bhimāno viśiṣṭebhyo viśiṣṭaṃ yo 'bhimanyate ||
