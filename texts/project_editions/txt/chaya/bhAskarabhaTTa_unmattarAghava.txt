ārya, iyamasmi | ko niyoga ājñāpayatu |

bahuvijñānakuśalaḥ khalveṣa sabhājanaḥ | kathamasya hṛdayamāvarjayituṃ śakyate |

bhavatu | yadanantarakaraṇīyaṃ tadāryo nirūpayatu |

	yasya hi madhuramātyo dakṣiṇapavano varūthinīnāthaḥ |
	priyabhramarānucaraḥ sa jayatu ciraṃ manmatho rājā ||

sakhi jānaki, agrata eva kusumāni dṛśyante | kimanyato dattadṛṣṭirgacchasi |

sakhi madhukarike, ito'pi puraḥsthite kānanāntare manoharāṇi kusumāni dṛśyante | tasmādabhilāṣeṇa gacchāmi |

sakhi jānaki, idānīmapyasmākamaparicitaḥ pradeśaḥ | kānaneṣu śāpānugrahasamarthā ṛṣayastiṣṭhanti | ato yasminakasminnapyāśrame kusumeṣu gṛhīteṣu yaḥ ko'pi kupyati | rāmabhadro'pi tava priyaṃ kartuṃ kanakamṛgaṃ gṛhītvā mṛgayāvyāpārādidānīmeva nivartiṣyate | tasmādetāvadbhiḥ kusumairnivartāvahe |

sarvajanasādhāraṇāni khalu vanalatāḥ phalapuṣpamūlāni nadījalāni ca | ataḥ kiṃnimittaṃ yaḥ ko'pi kupyati | āryaputrasya devārcanopayuktāni kusumāni paryāptāni | itaḥ puraḥsthite kānanāntare manoharāṇi kusumānyabhigṛhyoṭajaṃ gacchāvaḥ |

sakhi, ito bhramaraḥ kiṃnimittaṃ saṃbhramaṃsteṣu teṣu latāviṭapeṣu kusumakoṭareṣvāluṭhanparibhramati |

sakhi, makarandapānaniścalapakṣatayā pallavāntaritāmimāṃ sahacarīṃ bhramarīmaprekṣamāṇo'vilokanavedanayā nūnamunmattaḥ saṃvṛttaḥ |

sakhi, sādhu lakṣitaṃ tvayā |

āścaryam | saviśeṣaṃ paribhramannapyeṣa sahacarīṃ na paśyati |

madhukarike, saṃyojayainam |

anena sahakāradaṇḍena mañjarīṃ tāḍayitvā makarandapānaniścalāṃ bhramarīmutthāpayāmi | āścaryam | mañjaryā utthitayā tayā sahacaryā saṃgato'yaṃ madhukaro mādhavīmaṇḍapamadhiruhya madhurasaṃ pibansukhaṃ vartate | kutra gatā jānakī | āścaryam | na kutrāpi dṛśyate | āścaryam | iyaṃ manoharākṛtiḥ kāpi hariṇī snigdhayā dṛṣṭyā māṃ dṛṣṭvā niḥsaradbhirnayanasalilaiḥ sakhīsnehaṃ sūcayati | mama hṛdayamapyetasyāṃ ciraparicitamiva visrabdhaṃ bhavati | athavānayā jananāntarasakhyā bhavitavyam | atra maharṣerāśramaprabhāvādduṣṭamṛgāṇāṃ saṃcāro nāsti | tasmātkutra prasthitā jānakīti ma āścaryaṃ bhavati | mandabhāginyahaṃ khalu | mṛgayāvyāpārānnivṛttasya rāmabhadrasya kathaṃ kathayiṣye | rāmabhadro'pi śrutvā kīdṛśamavasthāntaramanubhaviṣyatīti vepate me hṛdayam |

itastato 'pi saṃbhramantastāpasajanā dṛśyante | ato rāmabhadro'pyāgacchatīti tarkayāmi |

aye, kanakamṛgaṃ gṛhītvāntikasthena lakṣmaṇena kimapi mantrayanrāmabhadra evāgacchati | ahamasya mārgaṃ parihṛtya tiṣṭhāmi |

sarvathā yadākadāpi kathanīya eva sa vṛttāntaḥ | tadgacchāmi | idānīmeva kathayāmi |

jayatu mahārājaḥ |

deva, yuṣmākaṃ devārcanāyai uṭajasamīpasthitāsu latāsu kusumānyavacinomīti nirgatā |

manoharāṇi kusumāni dṛśyanta itīdaṃ kānanāntaraṃ praviṣṭā jānakī anveṣantyā mayā na dṛṣṭā |

tathā |

etābhyāṃ vilocanābhyāmāryaputro dṛṣṭa ityaho me bhāgadheyam |

jayatu jayatvāryaputraḥ |

jayatu jayatu mahārājaḥ |

sulabhakopaḥ khalveṣa maharṣiḥ śrūyate |

aho, atiniṣṭhuratā tāpasānām |

tasminneva samupasthitayā hariṇyā bhavitavyam |
